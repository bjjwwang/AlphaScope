How It Works

From stock ticker to AI signal in 3 steps

No code. No setup. No finance degree. Just type a ticker.

1

Pick a stock

Type any ticker — AAPL, TSLA, NVDA. We support 600+ US stocks. Choose day-trading or swing-trading style.

2

LightGBM, LSTM, Transformer, GATs and more — all train on your stock's data. GPU-accelerated, ~10 min total.

Why Trust AI Signals?

We show our work. Every number is verifiable.

Unlike black-box signal services, AlphaScout is fully transparent.

Out-of-sample testing only

The model never sees the test data during training. Every return you see is real predictive performance, not curve-fitting. We show exact train/validation/test date ranges.

22 models compete fairly

Every model gets the same data, same features, same test period. The best model wins on merit. You can see each model's individual performance, win rate, and trade count.

Excess return, not just return

We always show buy-and-hold as a baseline. A model that returns +10% when the stock went up +15% actually lost. We highlight who beat the market, not who just rode it.

Built on Microsoft Qlib

AlphaScout is powered by Qlib, Microsoft's open-source quantitative research platform used by institutional quants. This isn't a toy — it's the same engine the pros use.
